🌫️ 10 Ways to Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

Simple actions you can start today to lower your environmental impact.

🌍 Why Reducing Carbon Footprint Matters

Your carbon footprint represents the total greenhouse gas emissions caused by your daily activities. Reducing it helps slow climate change and protects the environment for future generations.

✅ 10 Practical Ways

  1. Use energy-efficient appliances
  2. Switch to LED lighting
  3. Reduce air conditioning usage
  4. Walk, cycle, or use public transport
  5. Eat less processed food
  6. Reduce food waste
  7. Use reusable products
  8. Save water
  9. Plant trees or support green projects
  10. Track your emissions regularly
