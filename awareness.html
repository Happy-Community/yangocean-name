🌍 Environmental Awareness

Understanding the problem is the first step toward real change.

🌱 Why Environmental Awareness Matters

Environmental awareness helps people understand how daily actions affect the planet. From energy use to waste generation, every choice has an impact on climate and ecosystems.

When people are informed, they are more likely to adopt sustainable habits and support green policies.

📚 Key Environmental Issues

  • Climate change and global warming
  • Plastic pollution in oceans
  • Carbon emissions and footprint
  • Deforestation and habitat loss
  • Water scarcity and pollution

🌫️ Climate Change

Climate change is caused by greenhouse gas emissions such as CO₂ from human activities. It leads to rising temperatures, extreme weather, and sea level rise.

🧴 Plastic Pollution

Plastic waste harms marine life and ecosystems. Reducing plastic use is critical for protecting oceans.

Understanding your environmental footprint helps you make better decisions.
