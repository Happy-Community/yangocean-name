🌍 Environmental Awareness

Understand how daily choices affect the planet — and how small actions create big change.

🌱 What is Environmental Awareness?

Environmental awareness means understanding how human activities affect the planet, including pollution, resource consumption, waste generation, and climate change.

When we understand these issues, we can make better choices that protect nature and future generations.

📚 Key Environmental Issues

  • Plastic pollution in oceans and landfills
  • Water scarcity and overuse
  • Carbon emissions and global warming
  • Deforestation and biodiversity loss
  • Overconsumption of natural resources

🔍 Understand the 3 Major Impacts

🧴 Plastic Pollution

Plastic takes hundreds of years to break down and harms marine life and ecosystems.
