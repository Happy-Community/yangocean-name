🧴 Reduce Plastic Waste

Understand the problem and take action to protect the environment.

🌍 Why Environmental Awareness Matters

Understanding environmental issues helps us make better daily decisions. From energy use to waste, every action affects the planet.

When awareness increases, people naturally adopt more sustainable habits.

📚 Key Environmental Issues

  • Climate change and global warming
  • Plastic pollution in oceans
  • Carbon emissions and footprint
  • Deforestation and habitat loss
  • Water scarcity and pollution

🌫️ Climate Change

Climate change is driven by greenhouse gas emissions from human activities. It causes rising temperatures, extreme weather, and environmental disruption.

🧴 Plastic Pollution

Plastic waste harms marine life and ecosystems. It takes hundreds of years to decompose. Reducing plastic use is one of the most effective actions individuals can take.

✅ Practical Ways to Reduce Plastic

  • Use reusable bags and bottles
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Buy in bulk to reduce packaging
  • Recycle properly
  • Choose eco-friendly alternatives
