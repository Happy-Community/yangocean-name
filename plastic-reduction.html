🧴 Reduce Plastic Waste

Understand the problem and take action to protect the environment.

🌍 Why Environmental Awareness Matters

Understanding environmental issues helps us make better daily decisions. Every action has an impact on the planet.

As awareness increases, people naturally develop more sustainable habits.

📚 Key Environmental Issues

  • Climate change and global warming
  • Plastic pollution in oceans
  • Carbon emissions
  • Deforestation and habitat loss
  • Water scarcity and pollution

🌫️ Climate Change

Climate change is caused by greenhouse gas emissions, leading to rising temperatures and extreme weather.

🧴 Plastic Pollution

Plastic waste harms marine life and ecosystems, and can take hundreds of years to decompose.

✅ Practical Actions

  • Use reusable bags and bottles
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Buy in bulk
  • Recycle properly
  • Choose eco-friendly alternatives

💧 Reducing plastic is closely linked to water conservation.
