🌿 Eco Living

Simple daily habits that reduce waste, save resources, and improve your lifestyle.

🌱 What is Eco Living?

Eco living is about applying sustainable habits in your daily life. It focuses on practical actions that reduce waste, save energy, and use resources more responsibly.

✅ Daily Sustainable Habits

  • Switch off lights and appliances when not in use
  • Bring reusable bags, bottles, and containers
  • Avoid unnecessary purchases
  • Plan meals to reduce food waste
  • Recycle properly and consistently

⚡ Save Energy

  • Use natural light during the day
  • Switch to LED lighting
  • Reduce air conditioner usage
  • Unplug devices when not in use

👉 Learn Carbon Impact

💧 Save Water

  • Take shorter showers
  • Turn off tap while brushing teeth
  • Do not leave water running unnecessarily
  • Reuse water for plants when possible

♻️ Reduce Waste

  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Reuse items instead of throwing away
  • Recycle correctly
  • Buy products with less packaging

🍽️ Mindful Eating

  • Eat natural and less processed food
  • Finish your meals to reduce waste
  • Buy only what you need
  • Avoid overconsumption

🧘 Five Precepts & Eco Living

The Five Precepts are simple ethical guidelines that support a peaceful mind and a responsible way of living. They also naturally align with eco-friendly and sustainable living.

  • 1. Avoid harming living beings – Practice compassion and respect for all life, including animals and nature.
  • 2. Avoid taking what is not given – Respect resources and avoid wasteful consumption.
  • 3. Avoid harmful speech – Promote harmony, understanding, and positive communication.
  • 4. Avoid sexual misconduct – Maintain respect and responsibility in relationships and social behavior.
  • 5. Avoid intoxicants – Stay mindful and clear in decisions that affect health and the environment.

💚 Living with these principles naturally reduces harm to both people and the planet.
